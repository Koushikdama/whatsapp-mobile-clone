## Account Privacy Settings

### Making Your Account Private

1. **Navigate to Settings**
   - Tap the **Settings** icon in the bottom navigation (mobile)
   - Or click your profile picture in the top-left (desktop)

2. **Access Privacy Settings**
   - Tap **Privacy** from the settings menu

3. **Toggle Private Account**
   - Use the switch to turn **Private Account** ON
   - Your account is now private!

### What Happens When Your Account is Private?

When your account is private:
- ‚úÖ You control who can follow you
- ‚úÖ Only approved followers can see your profile
- ‚úÖ Only approved followers can message you
- ‚úÖ Only approved followers can see your status updates
- ‚úÖ You must approve all follow requests

### Making Your Account Public

1. Go to **Settings ‚Üí Privacy**
2. Toggle **Private Account** OFF
3. Your account is now public!

When your account is public:
- ‚úÖ Anyone can follow you instantly
- ‚úÖ Anyone can see your profile
- ‚úÖ Anyone can send you messages
- ‚úÖ Anyone can see your status updates
- ‚ùå No follow request approval needed

## Managing Follow Requests

### Viewing Follow Requests

**On Mobile:**
1. Tap your profile picture or go to **Settings**
2. Look for the **Follow Requests** notification badge
3. Tap **Follow Requests** to view pending requests

**On Desktop/Tablet:**
1. Navigate to **Settings** or your profile
2. Click **Follow Requests** from the menu
3. View all pending requests in tabs

### Request Tabs

#### Incoming Requests
Shows users who want to follow you:
- View user profiles by tapping their card
- **Accept** - Allow them to follow you
- **Reject** - Decline their request
- **Accept All** - Approve all pending requests (batch action)
- **Reject All** - Decline all pending requests (batch action)

#### Outgoing Requests
Shows your pending follow requests to private accounts:
- View which users you've requested to follow
- **Cancel** - Withdraw your follow request
- Track request status

### Search Functionality

Use the search bar to:
- Find specific follow requests
- Filter by username
- Quickly locate a user

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Can I switch between private and public accounts?**  
A: Yes! You can toggle between private and public anytime in Privacy Settings.

**Q: Will my existing followers be removed when I make my account private?**  
A: No, your existing followers remain. Only new follow attempts require approval.

**Q: Can I see who viewed my profile?**  
A: This feature is not currently available.

### Follow Requests

**Q: How long do follow requests last?**  
A: Follow requests remain pending until you accept, reject, or the sender cancels them.

**Q: Can I cancel a follow request I sent?**  
A: Yes! Go to Follow Requests ‚Üí Outgoing tab ‚Üí Tap Cancel.

**Q: What happens if I reject a follow request?**  
A: The user can send another request in the future. They are not notified of rejection.

**Q: Can I accept multiple requests at once?**  
A: Yes! Use the "Accept All" button for incoming requests.

### Privacy & Blocking

**Q: Can blocked users still see my messages in group chats?**  
A: Group chat behavior depends on your implementation. Typically, blocked users can still see group messages.

**Q: Will blocking someone delete our chat history?**  
A: No, blocking doesn't delete messages. You can delete the chat separately.

**Q: Can I message someone who has a private account?**  
A: Only if they've accepted your follow request.

### Status Updates

**Q: Who can see my status if my account is private?**  
A: Only users you've approved as followers can see your status updates.

**Q: Can I hide my status from specific followers?**  
A: You can set status privacy to "Selected Contacts" in Status Privacy Settings.

## Privacy Tips

### Best Practices

1. **Review Requests Regularly**
   - Check your follow requests weekly
   - Don't leave requests pending too long

2. **Be Selective**
   - Only approve people you know or want to connect with
   - Use the profile view before accepting

3. **Use Blocking Wisely**
   - Block users who spam or harass you
   - Remember: blocking is reversible

4. **Regular Privacy Audits**
   - Review your followers list monthly
   - Remove followers you no longer want to connect with
   - Check blocked users list periodically

### Security Recommendations

- ‚úÖ Use a strong account password
- ‚úÖ Enable two-factor authentication (if available)
- ‚úÖ Don't accept requests from unknown users
- ‚úÖ Report abusive behavior
- ‚úÖ Keep your app updated
